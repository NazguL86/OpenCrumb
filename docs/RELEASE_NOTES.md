# Release Notes - Open Crumb v1.0

## Initial Release

Welcome to Open Crumb - your companion for artisan bread baking!

### Features
- 27 authentic Italian bread recipes including pizza, focaccia, ciabatta, and baguettes
- Dough calculator to scale recipes for any number of portions
- 7 comprehensive guides on bread-making techniques
- Support for 6 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese
- Beautiful images for every recipe and guide
- Tablet-optimized layouts with grid view
- Completely offline - no internet required
- No ads, no tracking, respects your privacy

### Recipes Include
- Pizza doughs (same-day, 8-hour, extended fermentation, with poolish)
- Focaccia varieties (Genovese, with poolish, with toppings)
- Ciabatta (with biga, with poolish)
- Baguettes (direct method, with poolish)
- White bread and specialty toppings

### Guides Cover
- Pre-ferments (Poolish and Biga)
- Hand kneading techniques
- Stretch and fold method
- Shaping techniques
- Fermentation basics
- Achieving open crumb structure
