Privacy Policy for Open Crumb

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Overview

Open Crumb is a recipe-keeping application that respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Open Crumb does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All recipe data is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All recipes, guides, and calculator data are stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

Open Crumb does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

External Links

The app may contain links to external websites (such as Amazon for purchasing the companion book). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Children's Privacy

Open Crumb does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's listing on the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
